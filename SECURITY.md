# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Agency Workload is pre-alpha and not approved for production use. Security fixes apply to the latest
development line until the first stable release defines a support window.

## Reporting A Vulnerability

Do not open a public issue. Email `security@brainai.team` with:

- The affected commit or version
- Reproduction steps
- Impact and prerequisites
- Any suggested mitigation

Do not include real credentials or customer data. BrainAI Team will acknowledge a complete report
within three business days and provide a status update within seven business days.

## Scope

Authentication, authorization, data isolation, CSV handling, email delivery, dependencies,
containers, and public-release processes are in scope. Social engineering, denial-of-service testing
against public infrastructure, and accessing data that is not yours are prohibited.
